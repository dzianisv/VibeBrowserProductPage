We're excited to announce a new partnership that marks a significant step in our mission to bring AI browser automation to every product team.

## Meet Kateyan

[Kateyan](https://www.linkedin.com/in/kateyan) brings deep expertise in product development and go-to-market strategy. With years of experience helping companies scale their products, she understands the unique challenges that product teams face when adopting new AI technologies.

## The Vision

Product companies are sitting on a massive opportunity to deploy AI agents that can automate workflows, enhance user experiences, and reduce operational overhead. But the barrier to entry has been high — until now.

Through this partnership, we're building a new product specifically designed to help product companies:

- **Onboard OpenClaw agents** into their existing workflows
- **Integrate AI automation** without requiring engineering resources
- **Measure ROI** from day one with clear metrics and dashboards
- **Scale confidently** with enterprise-grade security and compliance

## Why This Matters

The AI agent ecosystem is growing rapidly, but most solutions are built for developers or individual users. Product companies — the teams building the software we all use every day — have been underserved.

This new product will change that. By combining OpenClaw's powerful browser automation capabilities with a product-led onboarding experience, we're making it possible for any product team to deploy AI agents in weeks, not months.

## What's Next

Over the coming months, we'll be:

1. **Building the onboarding platform** — A guided setup experience that gets product teams from sign-up to first working agent in under an hour
2. **Creating integration templates** — Pre-built connectors for popular product tools (CRM, support, analytics)
3. **Developing analytics dashboards** — Real-time visibility into agent performance, cost savings, and user engagement
4. **Launching a beta program** — Limited spots available for early adopter product companies

## Get Involved

If you're at a product company interested in early access to this new offering, we'd love to hear from you. We're looking for teams who are ready to be among the first to deploy AI agents at scale.

Reach out to discuss partnership opportunities or join the waitlist for our beta program.
